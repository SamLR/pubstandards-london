Pub Standards London

The infamous beer-infused, casual, social, warm, raucous, fine, dandy, monthly London meet-up for web folk.

Keeping in touch

iCal feed

Never forget a Pub Standards event by adding our iCal feed to your calendar! +

Mailing list

To keep abreast of the latest events, fun facts, and debates about what to call this months Substandards, sign up to the London Pubstandards mailing list.

Twitter

You can find us on twitter, posting infrequent reminders to come drinking with us.

IRC Channel

A lot of the Pub Standards regulars tend to hang out in our IRC chatroom - #pubstandards on irc.freenode.net. If you're not an IRC user, try the webchat interface, or IRCCloud.
