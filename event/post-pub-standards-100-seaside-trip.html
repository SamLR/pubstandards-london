Post-Pub Standards 100 Seaside Trip

Was held at Whitstable on Friday March 14, 2014

Since we've all got Friday booked off, instead of sitting around hung over and feeling sorry for ourselves, we're going to the seaside! The plan is to head to Whitstable, which is a nice place with plentiful seafood and a local brewery bar if we decide such a thing is necessary.

Getting the 12:22 high speed train from St Pancras (changing at Rochester) which takes about 90 minutes to get to Whitstable.

You can also go from Victoria, which costs slightly less and takes about the same time without a change.

And you can get on the same train at Stratford International if you're very East, it passes through at 12:28.
